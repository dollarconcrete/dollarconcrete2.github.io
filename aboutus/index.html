ABOUT US

Dollar Concrete Construction Company has a proud heritage of value and service in the concrete industry. More than 35 years ago, Jim Dollar founded this company on the basic principle of providing quality concrete work at a fair price. Since that time, Dollar Concrete has grown and expanded both their client base and their market reach, and continues to provide superior concrete construction services for our clients around the southeastern United States. Many of Dollar’s current clients have been so for more than 20 years.

In addition, Dollar Concrete routinely provides all of the reinforcing steel (rebar and welded wire mesh) for the concrete projects we construct. Through our on-site sister company, Premier Rebar, Inc., we also staff a full crew of in-house detailers, shop personnel, and trucks so as to detail, list, bend and ship all of the rebar for the majority of the projects we serve around the southeast. Our ability to directly control this aspect can significantly aid our clients by reducing material lead time and improving fast-track schedules.
