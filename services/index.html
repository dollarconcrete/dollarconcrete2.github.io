OUR SERVICES

Dollar Concrete Construction Company offers and performs a wide variety of services for all phases of concrete construction. From foundations, vaults and slabs, to walls, columns and elevated concrete, Dollar is equipped to provide a complete turn-key package for all phases of structural concrete work. Dollar is also equipped to furnish and install site concrete work including sidewalks, curbs, steps, and concrete paving.
